Glass, Light & Shadow

I set up a little studio and started messing around with bottles and lighting. No real goal, just playing with shadows, color, and how the light wraps around glass. Some shots came out warm and moody, others cold and sharp. I changed ISO, aperture, and distance, sometimes on purpose, sometimes just to see what would happen.

Each photo has a different vibe even though the subject stayed the same. That’s what I was after. Not the bottles — the way the light treats them.
