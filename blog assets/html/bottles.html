Glass, Light & Shadow

A sculptural lighting study using bottles as subjects.

These images were created by playing with ISO, aperture, and diffusion in a home studio environment — shifting between moods: editorial, surreal, and cinematic. Each photo explores transparency, distortion, and reflection. Light wraps. Shape fades. Nothing sits still.

Sometimes it’s not about the subject — it’s what the subject bends.
