Portraits

This series was just me and a camera and a couple lights. I wanted to see what changes when you keep everything the same but swap who’s in front of the lens. Same room, same setup. But each shot hits different depending on the vibe, pose, and light wrap. The smallest tweaks in framing or posture completely shift the image. Some of the strongest ones weren’t the most perfect — just the ones that felt right.
