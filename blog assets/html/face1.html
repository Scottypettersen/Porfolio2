Portraits

This quarter I spent time exploring portrait photography.

Same lights, same backdrop, same camera. But every person brought something different to the frame.

I focused on setups like head and shoulders framing, light wrapping, and classic 45 degree lighting.

A small change in lens, posture, or expression completely changed the final image.

The best ones weren’t always the most "correct." They just felt real.
